• Hold v and click to add new vertices.
  • Click vertices to select them.
  • Select a vertex, hold e and click another vertex to add an edge.
  • Hold g and click somewhere to move center of gravity.
  • Move vertices by dragging them around.
  • To remove a vertex or edge, select it and press r.
  • Select additional vertices or edges by holding CTRL and clicking.
  • Play around with the parameters on the left.
